# 💫 About Me:
👋 Hi, I'm Grega — a freelance full-stack developer and electrical engineering student at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FE) in Ljubljana, Slovenia.<br><br>I build and maintain production systems for clients, working across the stack with Node.js/Express, Next.js, MongoDB, Auth0, Docker, and nginx on AlmaLinux servers. A big chunk of my current work revolves around AI-powered automation — building agent workflows with n8n, RAG pipelines with vector search, MCP tool integrations, and Shopify data pipelines.<br><br>My background started with embedded systems and IoT (ESP32, Arduino, Modbus RTU), and that's still a passion — I've built everything from solar inverter integrations to roller shutter controllers. I'm comfortable moving between low-level C/C++ and full-stack JavaScript/TypeScript, and I enjoy the challenge of connecting hardware to cloud infrastructure.<br><br>Outside of code, I'm into alpine mountaineering in the Julian Alps 🏔️, photography, 3D printing (Anycubic Kobra 2 Plus), CAD work in Fusion 360 & Blender, woodworking, and video editing in DaVinci Resolve.<br><br>Always happy to collaborate or chat about embedded systems, AI automation, or full-stack development. Feel free to explore my repos or reach out!<br><br>
